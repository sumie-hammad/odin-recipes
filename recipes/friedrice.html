Special Fried Rice

Description

Colorful and tasty rice stir-fried with vegetables, eggs, and optionally shrimp or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Chicken or shrimp (optional)

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry veggies, add rice and soy sauce.
  3. Mix in eggs and protein.
  4. Serve hot.
