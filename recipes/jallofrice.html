Jollof Rice

Home

Description

A classic West African rice dish made with tomatoes, onions, and spices. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Oil, salt, and seasonings

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes and onions.
  2. Cook sauce, add seasoning.
  3. Add rice and water.
  4. Simmer until rice is done.
