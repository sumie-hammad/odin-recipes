Delicious Mandi Rice

Description

Mandi rice is a traditional Yemeni dish loved across the Middle East. It’s a fragrant rice dish cooked with a blend of spices and usually served with tender, slow-cooked meat.

This recipe captures the authentic flavor of Mandi rice, perfect for special occasions or a rich family meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces) or 500g lamb
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups chicken stock or water
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • Optional: raisins and fried almonds for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté onions until golden brown, then add garlic and cook briefly.
  2. Add all spices and stir for 1 minute to release aroma.
  3. Add chicken pieces and sear on all sides.
  4. Pour in stock or water. Cover and simmer until the chicken is cooked (20–30 minutes).
  5. Remove the chicken and set aside. Reserve the broth.
  6. Add washed rice to the broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Cover and cook the rice on low heat until tender and liquid is absorbed (15–20 minutes).
  8. Grill or bake the cooked chicken until slightly crispy.
  9. Fluff the rice and serve with the grilled chicken on top.
  10. Garnish with fried almonds and raisins if desired.
